Hajoon "HJ" Yun

Founder, President, Chief Reporter

"A capacity to change is indispensable. Equally indispensable is the capacity to hold fast to that which is good."

-John Foster Dulles, 52nd US Secretary of State

This is the guiding quote for my journey.

As an academically advanced student, I pride myself on being up to date on all the world's current events. My passion for international relations and politics began long before my academic career. Even in elementary school, I had many questions for my teacher about who ran the country. As I started high school, I began to mature this passion and dig deep into the world of international relations. I have an academic record of not only taking government classes but also excelling in these classes. Over the summer between my sophomore and junior years, I had the privilege of attending Georgetown University's Summer Academy for International Relations. After returning from a wonderful 3-week excursion in Washington, DC, I was more motivated to pursue a career as a diplomat. Through ISM, I will display my depth of passion for international affairs and diplomacy. Through the YUNity Project, I aim to be at the forefront of the effort to reclaim our country from hyperpolarization and restore a period of calm, diplomatic governance.
