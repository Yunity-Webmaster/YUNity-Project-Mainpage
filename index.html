OPINION: Why We SHOULD Talk Politics on Thanksgiving

Why should people not be afraid of bringing up politics at dinner?

"In a democracy, truth and education stand as pillars. When these pillars are threatened, our democracy is threatened. In a society where extremism has become mainstream, people must be able to realize that a difference in opinion is inevitable and must be well-managed. The founders of the United States framed our government with civil discourse at its core. The greatest and brightest moments in our nation's history were those marked by compromise. A willingness to have an open mind is the essential ingredient in the great American experiment. The YUNity Project is more than just another organization; it's a mission that anybody and everybody can join in this pursuit for truth. I hope that even in the darkest of times, we can realize the YUNity Project's motto: veritas vos liberabit, the truth shall set you free."
