About Us

Welcome to The YUNity Project. Our mission is to unite our divided society by developing carefully curated research writings that focus on the facts.

Founded in 2025, by a team of young-adults, the senior leadership is focused on bringing you unbiased research articles, opinion pieces, emergency statements, podcast episodes, and public interviews.

At The YUNity Project, we are committed to our motto "Veritas vos liberabit" "...the truth shall set you free". We promise to "tell the truth, the whole truth, and nothing but the truth, so help us god" (California Oath of Sworn Testimony).

We achieve the truth by conducting nonpartisan research about specific policy areas. We ensure nonpartisanship by having the Independent Publication Commission (IPC) wing of The YUNity Project vote on research articles and emergency statements to maintain a sense of truth and ensure the absence of bias within our works.

Anonymous Contributors

*Why are some names and identities pseudonyms? Although the YUNity Project believes in nonpartisan, centrist journalism and politics, the current state of politics has led some members on staff to request that their identities remain anonymous. The YUNity Project will always put the safety of our staff above all else. Let this serve as a reminder, politics - especially in America - should never be a field where people are afraid to speak up for their beliefs. The extremism on both sides has led to this unfortunate decision. Nevertheless, the YUNity Project will continue to provide balanced, fact-driven journalism and research to all communities.
