About Us

Welcome to YungPolitics, your go-to source for insightful coverage of domestic and international politics. Our mission is to present clear, unbiased reporting that empowers readers to make informed decisions and engage meaningfully with the political process.

Founded in 2024 by a team of passionate writers and political enthusiasts, we aim to break down complex political events and provide context that matters. From in-depth analysis of federal policy to local governance and global affairs, we strive to cover the stories shaping our world.

At YungPolitics, we believe that knowledge is power, and transparency is essential. We are committed to bringing you accurate, engaging, and thought-provoking content while maintaining integrity and professionalism in everything we do.

Anonymous Contributors

*Why are some names and identities pseudonyms? Although the YUNity Project believes in nonpartisan, centrist journalism and politics, the current state of politics has led some members on staff to request that their identities remain anonymous. The YUNity Project will always put the safety of our staff above all else. Let this serve as a reminder, politics - especially in America - should never be a field where people are afraid to speak up for their beliefs. The extremism on both sides has led to this unfortunate decision. Nevertheless, the YUNity Project will continue to provide balanced, fact-driven journalism and research to all communities.
